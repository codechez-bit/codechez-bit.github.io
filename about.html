Who we are and what we do:

Founded in 2013, Benzer Industries is a significant and integral part in the tissue paper industry. We were built from the ground up with the goal of providing cost-friendly hygiene products in a fast growing industry. We want to be one of the world’s foremost manufacturers for tissue products: starting with rolling out to Nigeria and expanding to other ECOWAS countries.

MISSION STATEMENT

We strive to achieve mutually beneficial relationships with our customers/partners. Together, we can achieve market leadership by providing tissue-based hygiene products extensively and sustainably.

HISTORY OF COMPANY

Since 2013, we have been investing our time and effort in R&D towards supplying the best products we possibly can.

After spending this time trying to figure out the most appropriate strategy to manufacture and distribute tissue products in Nigeria, (and eventually the rest of the ECOWAS countries) we feel that we are able to create products that can compete with the market leaders of tissue production in Nigeria.

With the help of our customers and partners, we hope to deliver products of value and efficiency that can reach a global scale.

VALUE PROPOSITION

CUSTOMER FOCUS – We manufacture products that cater to our customers’ needs and desires to the best of our ability. Our research has proved that given a soft feel and high absorbing ability, our customers would greatly appreciate a product that can deliver durability. The attractive packaging we provide has also proved to catch the consumer’s eye from a line-up of other tissue brands.

COMPETITIVE PRICING – At a price designed to appeal to the maximum percent of consumers of the tissue market, our products are forecasted to be easily accepted by customers looking for a cheaper and more durable option.

SUSTAINABILITY – Though tissue production is a difficult issue to combat in an industry where environmental standards are hard to reach, we have been able to provide products with renewable materials to the best of our ability.
